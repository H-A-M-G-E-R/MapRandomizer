Screen flashing

This setting affects the graphical appearance of screen flashing, e.g., during the lightning storm at the landing site, during the escape sequence, bosses, and power bombs.

  • Vanilla: Screen flashing happens as in the vanilla game.

  • Reduced: Most flashes to bright colors are reduced to be a milder change in brightness, but still noticable. This is done on a best-effort basis, and individual photosensitivity to these effects may still vary.
